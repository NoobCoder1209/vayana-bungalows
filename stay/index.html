About the bungalows

A handful of villas on a quiet stretch of the Bulgarian Black Sea coast, each with its own light and its own view.

The three Vayana bungalows share a coastline but are framed by very different landscapes — one looking out to the open sea, one tucked into the pine ridge, one a step from the sand. Each is hand-finished in local materials and styled for slow days: long mornings on the terrace, evenings that end early, salt air through every open window.

Pick the one whose view you'd rather wake up to. If you'd like a recommendation, just send an enquiry — we'll help you choose.
