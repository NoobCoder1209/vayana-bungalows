Welcome to Vayana Bungalows

On the Black Sea coast, at the edge of the Strandzha forest.

Vayana Bungalows is a small family-run retreat in Tsarevo, Bulgaria — a handful of hardwood bungalows tucked between the pines and the sea, hand-built and run with the same care we'd put into our own home.

Our guests come for the quiet, the daybreak swims, and the kind of unhurried hospitality that you remember long after the drive back.
