Inside the villa

A hardwood retreat, open to the Pacific.

Set on a low rise above the lagoon, the Premier Oceanview Villa is a single-level pavilion of local hardwoods, with a deep timber deck, a salt-water plunge pool, and louvered walls that fold back to the trade winds.

Inside, a king bed faces the sea through a wall of glass; a freestanding stone tub looks the same direction. Mornings begin with the sun coming up through the palms; evenings end with the lagoon's soft phosphorescence under the stars.

Every Premier Oceanview Villa includes a dedicated butler, twice-daily housekeeping, and a private outrigger berth at the resort dock for guests who'd like to take to the water on their own schedule.
