Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Cancellation Policy explains what happens if you need to cancel or modify a reservation at Vayana Bungalows. It is part of, and should be read together with, our Terms & Conditions.

This page is a draft. Every figure rendered in a yellow highlight is a working assumption (lead-time threshold, refund percentage, SLA, currency) and MUST be reviewed and replaced before this site goes live to paying guests.

1. Last updated

This Policy was last updated on 22 June 2026. See §9 Contact for who to email if anything below is unclear.

2. Cancellation tiers

If you need to cancel a confirmed reservation, the refund you receive depends on how many days before your check-in date we receive the written cancellation request:

  • More than 30 days before check-in — we refund 100% of any amount you have paid. Free cancellation tier.
  • Between 30 and 14 days before check-in — we refund 50% of any amount you have paid. Partial-refund tier.
  • Less than 14 days before check-in — we refund 0%. No-refund tier.

The lead time is counted from the date and time we receive your written cancellation (see §3), not the date you sent it. Tiers apply to the total amount you have paid us toward the reservation, including any deposit described in §5 of the Terms & Conditions. Whether the deposit is refundable on the same schedule, or is non-refundable regardless of tier, is to be confirmed before launch.

3. How to cancel

To cancel a reservation, please email us at contact@vayanabungalows.com with your booking reference (sent in the confirmation email), the dates of your reservation, and a short note that you wish to cancel.

We will acknowledge your cancellation in writing within 48 hours on working days, confirm the applicable refund tier per §2, and let you know the timeline for the refund per §7. Until you have received that written acknowledgement from us, please consider the cancellation as pending.

4. Modifications

Date changes and guest-count changes are treated differently from full cancellations and are usually handled at no charge if requested far enough in advance:

  • Date changes. Requests received more than 14 days before the original check-in date, for new dates of equal length and the same bungalow, are free of charge subject to availability. Closer to check-in, or for longer stays / different bungalows, a price difference or fee may apply. Exact change-fee schedule to be confirmed before launch.
  • Guest-count changes. Adding or removing guests within the bungalow's stated occupancy limit is free of charge; we ask you to let us know in advance so we can prepare the bungalow accordingly. Exceeding the occupancy limit is not permitted; see §8 House rules of the Terms & Conditions.

If a modification cannot be accommodated and you choose to cancel instead, the cancellation tier in §2 applies based on the original check-in date.

5. No-shows

If you do not arrive on your check-in date and have not cancelled in writing before the check-in time, the reservation is treated as a no-show. The default refund for a no-show is 0%.

If circumstances beyond your control prevent you from arriving, please contact us as soon as you can — see §6 Force majeure for how we handle exceptions.

6. Force majeure

Sometimes events beyond either party's control — natural disasters, sudden travel restrictions, or regional emergencies — make a stay impossible. In those cases we will work with you in good faith to find a fair outcome, which may include moving the dates free of charge, holding the amount you have paid as credit toward a future stay, or issuing a refund outside the standard tiers in §2.

The legal basis for force majeure (what qualifies, who bears which costs, and how the refund is calculated) is set out in §10 of the Terms & Conditions. This Policy and those Terms should be read together. Exact list of qualifying events and standard outcome to be finalised with legal review.

7. Refund processing

Once the refund tier is confirmed under §2 (or under §6 for force majeure), refunds are issued within 10 working days of our written cancellation acknowledgement.

Refunds are issued in EUR to the same payment method you used to pay the original deposit and balance. If that is not possible (for example, the card has expired), we will contact you to agree an alternative method. Whether bank or card-processing fees are deducted from the refund is to be confirmed before launch.

8. Disputes

If you disagree with how this Policy has been applied to your reservation, please first contact us at the email in §3 and describe what you would like us to reconsider. We will respond in writing and try to find a resolution within 10 working days.

If we cannot reach an agreement, the dispute-resolution and governing-law provisions in §11 of the Terms & Conditions apply, including the courts named there. Nothing in this Policy limits any consumer rights you may have under applicable mandatory law.
