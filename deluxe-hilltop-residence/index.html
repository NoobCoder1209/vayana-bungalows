Inside the bungalow

A two-level pavilion above the lagoon.

Bungalow 2 is a two-level hardwood pavilion set into the slope above the lagoon. The lower floor opens to a long infinity pool that reads, from the water, as a single sheet to the horizon; the upper floor is the private bedroom, with the island falling away in every direction.

A chef's table on the deck seats six and is paired with a kitchen for in-residence dining; on most evenings the resort's executive chef will cook for guests on request, with a single-day notice. The view shifts every half hour from the trade-wind palms to the open sea.

Bungalow 2 includes a dedicated butler, twice-daily housekeeping, and an electric buggy for the run down to the beach club. Children are welcome; the second bedroom converts to twin beds on request.
